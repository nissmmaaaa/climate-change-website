The Climate Change Story: A Journey Through Time

Explore the impact of climate change from the Industrial Revolution to today

The Beginning of the Problem

Late 18th Century: Industrial Revolution

Birth of fossil fuel use; coal and oil become primary energy sources.

Early 19th Century: Initial Rise in CO₂ Levels

Fossil fuel combustion increases CO₂ linking emissions to industrial activity.

Growth of Greenhouse Gas Emissions

Urbanization and industrialization increased the concentration of methane, nitrous oxide, and carbon dioxide in the atmosphere.

Impact on the Planet

The impact of climate change is evident in extreme weather events, biodiversity loss, and economic costs.
